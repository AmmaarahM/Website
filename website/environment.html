E-Waste

e-waste refers to discarded electronic devices

technology is often difficult to repair,so often it's cheaper to replace a

product than to repair it, this contributes to e-waste

many consumers will replace working devices with a newer model each year,

this also produces unncessary e-waste

e-waste includes toxic chemicals,

there are strict rules as to how e-waste should be disposed

However, many organisations bypasss the rules by sending the e-waste to coutries with fewer regulations

which affects their land and water supplies, which in turn affects the wildlife and the population
