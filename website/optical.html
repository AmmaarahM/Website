Facial Recognition

Definition:

a method of identifying/ confirming an individual's identitiy using their face

it works by matching the faces of people walking past cameras to images of people on a watch list

the watch lists can contain poictures of anyone, incluing people who are not suspected of any wrongdoing

Disadvantages:

suveillance - people worry that the use of facial recognition creates the potential for mass surveillance,

which could restrict an individuals freedom, whilst the technology could be used to trackdown criminals,

it could also allow them to track down ordinary and innocent people at any time

scope for error - a slight chaange in camera angle or a change in appearance could lead to errors

In 2018, it was reported that Amazon's facial recognition technolody had falsely identified

28 memebers of the US Congress as poeple arrested for crimes
